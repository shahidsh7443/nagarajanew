PATHS: The Pathways learning experience has 10 Paths. Each Path has its own set of Required and Elective Projects, and each Path was developed around the five core competencies mentioned above. The 10 Paths are: Dynamic Leadership, Effective Coaching, Innovative Planning, Leadership Development, Motivational Strategies, Persuasive Influence, Presentation Mastery, Strategic Relationships, Team Collaboration, and Visionary Communication. Each Path is represented on the website and in various TI publications with its own icon, shown below.
