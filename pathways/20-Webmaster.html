Frank Storey, DTM4

fstorey1943@gmail.com
 

I'm a retired Electrical Engineer who worked on the radar in fighter planes for 31 years, including 5 years in the Netherlands and five years aboard various US aircraft carriers (USS Enterprise, USS America, USS Saratoga, USS Oriskany, and USS Constellation).

I joined the Singularly Speaking Toastmasters Club in June 1986 in Fort Worth, Texas and was a member of 5 clubs within a month...and I never looked back. January 1991 I was transferred from Texas to Baltimore, and joined a club in District 27 (Lone Star Toastmasters) and then one in District 18 (Gaveliers). Currently I'm a member and mentor to Nu Genesis Toastmasters Club and two prison Toastmasters Gavel Clubs (for 12 years).

At my first District function, a judging workshop, I met the woman who would become my wife, Karen Kidwell Storey, DTM2. We have been married 20 years, both love Toastmasters and love to travel.
